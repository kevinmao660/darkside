What is the Dark Side?

The two major forces of the Star Wars galaxy include the Dark side and the Light Side. The Dark Side focuses on taking over the Light Side and the rest of the Star Wars galaxy. The Dark Side has very powerful contributors named the siths, who are also the founders. Siths control the dark side of the force and bend it to use for their purposes. The Force is an ability some special beings are born with that allow them to levitate and control objects with their minds rather than their hands. It allows them to a number of different things that normal people can't. Special abilities that seperate siths from normal people are...

  • Force lightning

  • Force Choke

  • Using the force

  • Special jumping abilities
